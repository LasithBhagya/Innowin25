About Innowin '25

Where Ideas Spark. Invention Ignites.

Innowin '25 is the official invention exhibition of Mahanama College, where curiosity meets creativity and bold ideas take center stage. This year’s exhibition brings together the brightest young minds from across the school—and beyond—to showcase cutting-edge inventions, ground-breaking research, and bold concepts across science, technology, sustainability, health, and more.

From interactive exhibits and live demos to thought-provoking workshops and challenges, Innowin '25 is not just an event—it’s a movement. Whether you're a student, inventor, or curious mind, join us as we explore, invent, and inspire the next generation of thinkers and changemakers.

  • Open to individuals and teams
  • Multiple categories covering all fields of Invention
  • Judged by industry experts

Frequently Asked Questions

Inventors from grade 1-13 are welcome to participate in the event. All students from Mahanama College are particularly encouraged to join.
We accept all types of inventions across various fields including technology, healthcare, environmental solutions, consumer products, and more. The invention must be original, and while prototypes are preferred, well-documented concepts with clear implementation plans are also acceptable.
Submissions go through two rounds of evaluation: initial screening by our technical team, and evaluation by category-specific expert panels.
