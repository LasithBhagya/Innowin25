About The Competition

Celebrating Innovation

InnovateX is the premier global competition for inventors, innovators, and creative thinkers. Our mission is to recognize and reward groundbreaking ideas that have the potential to shape the future.

Since our inception in 2010, we've helped launch over 200 inventions into successful products and businesses. This year's competition promises to be our biggest yet, with expanded categories and increased prize money.

  • Open to individuals and teams worldwide
  • Multiple categories covering all fields of innovation
  • Judged by industry experts and successful inventors

Frequently Asked Questions

The competition is open to inventors of all ages and backgrounds worldwide. Individuals, teams, startups, and even established companies with new inventions are welcome to participate. Student inventors are particularly encouraged to join.
We accept all types of inventions across various fields including technology, healthcare, environmental solutions, consumer products, and more. The invention must be original, and while prototypes are preferred, well-documented concepts with clear implementation plans are also acceptable.
Submissions go through three rounds of evaluation: initial screening by our technical team, evaluation by category-specific expert panels, and final judging by our prestigious jury. The exhibition event includes public voting which contributes to special recognition awards.
